A ligma enjoyer.

This aside, true to his profile, Shu Yamino is a kind-hearted sorcerer - undoubtedly NIJISANJI EN's pillar of tech "shupport". However, though he possesses great knowledge in technology, he also holds an unhealthy amount of knowledge regarding Twitch lingo, exhibits an obsession with "deez nuts" and "ligma balls" jokes and seldom allows his body to make contact with grass.

Despite this, not only is the sorcerer endlessly supportive of his friends, often visiting the other livers' chats and proactively arranging collabs - even from outside of NIJISANJI - he has proven to also be a reliable senpai (despite his visible cringe from being unprepared to be a senpai) Noticeably, when his kouhai from Noctyx, Yugo Asuma and Sonny Brisko, were having trouble in their streams, they looked to Shu for help.

History

Background

On 18 June 2021, NIJISANJI EN opened two sets of auditions, one of which was specifically for male Livers. The male-specific auditions lasted until 1 August.

On 17 December 2021, NIJISANJI EN announced the upcoming debut of its fourth wave "Luxiem", accompanied by a teaser featuring the new Livers' designs and voices.

Shu's YouTube channel was created on 1 December 2021, and his Twitter account also created in the same month. His first tweet was made on 17 December 2021.

Debut

Luxiem made their debuts on 20 December 2021 (JST) as part of a special debut program hosted on the NIJISANJI EN channel, with commentary provided by Elira Pendora and Oliver Evans. Shu was the second of the group to debut. Afterwards, all five members of Luxiem appeared on the main channel.
